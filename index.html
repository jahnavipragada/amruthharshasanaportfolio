Experiences

ML processor design internship at Ceremorphic

I worked as a part of the Deep Learning Research team. Our team aims to find a way that avoids the dense layer computation in Convolutional Neural Networks. I thought a new method could be replacing some kernels with 1x1 kernels and finally replacing the whole fully connected layers with Global Average Pooling and then fed into softmax directly. I have implemented the whole theory by working with VGG16, VGG19 on cifar10 first and then moved on to Resnet50 on the Imagenet dataset. Every updated model worked better than the state of the art model.
